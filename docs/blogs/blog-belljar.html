Our essays about the Bell Jar were supposed to be due today. Colin finished his... the rest of us however...

The time was not wasted though. We discussed how the 'performative' trend on social media was tightly linked to a lot of the ideas of self-identity and external perception that Esther wrestles with in the novel.

Over a dinner of home cooked chicken nuggets we brainstormed a list of second books to read and the big-picture future of our book club... big things coming!
